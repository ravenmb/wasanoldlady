(There was an Old Lady)...

The assignment for my Web Authoring class at CSU East Bay was to create a long scrolling
webpage at least 10,000 pixels long. During a brainstorming session I thought of telling
a children's story where the elements of the story built upon one another. There Was
An Old Lady Who Swallowed A Fly came to mind and I thought it had potential. Then I
sketched an evil looking old woman with her mouth stretched open impossibly wide and I
knew this was what I wanted to do.

The biggest challenge was getting all the elements to appear and disappear at the right moments
while scrolling. Once all the parts of the story worked correctly, I decided to add a "tally"
of the animals she had already eaten that would fly in from the side.
